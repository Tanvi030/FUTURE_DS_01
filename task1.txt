## 🔹 Part 1: Monthly Sales Trend

### Steps:

1. **Insert PivotTable**

   * Select your data range (including headers).
   * Go to **Insert → PivotTable**.
   * Place PivotTable on a **New Worksheet**.

2. **Set up Fields**

   * Drag **Order Date** → **Rows**.
   * Drag **Sales** → **Values** (make sure it shows **Sum of Sales**).

3. **Group Dates**

   * Right-click on any date in the **Row Labels**.
   * Choose **Group…**.
   * In the pop-up, check **Months** and **Years** → Click **OK**.

4. **Insert Chart**

   * With the PivotTable selected, go to **Insert → Line Chart → Line with Markers**.
   * Format chart (add Title: “Monthly Sales Trend”).

---

## 🔹 Part 2: Category & Sub-Category Performance

### Steps:

1. **Insert PivotTable** (or reuse the first one by duplicating).

   * Drag **Category** → **Rows**.
   * Drag **Sub-Category** → **Rows** (below Category).
   * Drag **Sales** → **Values**.
   * Drag **Profit** → **Values**.

2. **Insert Chart**

   * Select the PivotTable.
   * Go to **Insert → Column Chart → Clustered Column**.
   * Format chart (Chart Title: “Category & Sub-Category Sales & Profit”).

---

## 🔹 Part 3: Top 10 Products by Sales

### Steps:

1. **Insert PivotTable**

   * Drag **Product Name** → **Rows**.
   * Drag **Sales** → **Values**.

2. **Sort Data**

   * Right-click on any Sales value.
   * Choose **Sort → Sort Largest to Smallest**.

3. **Filter Top 10**

   * Right-click any Product Name → **Filter → Top 10…**.
   * In dialog box:

     * “Top” → “10” → “Items” → by **Sum of Sales**.

4. **Insert Chart**

   * With PivotTable selected, go to **Insert → Treemap Chart**.
   * Format chart (Chart Title: “Top 10 Products by Sales”).

---

✅ Now you’ll have:

* **Line Chart** = Monthly Sales Trend.
* **Clustered Column Chart** = Category & Sub-Category Performance.
* **Treemap** = Top 10 Products by Sales.
